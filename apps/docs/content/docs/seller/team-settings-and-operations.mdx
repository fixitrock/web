## Settings

The main settings route is:

`/@username/settings`

Use it for:

- Seller profile information
- Visual identity updates
- Username-linked business details
- General storefront maintenance

## Teams

The project contains a team area for seller operations.

If enabled for your role or deployment, use the team tools to:

- Add or search staff members
- Separate owner and counter responsibilities
- Keep storefront operations from depending on one device

## Stock and Operational Pages

Some seller roles also expose:

- `/@username/stocks`
- `/@username/teams`

Treat these as internal tools, not customer-facing routes.

## Routine Weekly Checklist

1. Review profile and storefront details.
2. Confirm key products are still priced correctly.
3. Check recent orders for anomalies.
4. Update categories or brands if the catalog changed.
5. Remove outdated demo or temporary items.

## Support and Escalation

If a seller is blocked by route access, missing data, or search/navigation issues:

1. Confirm the active session belongs to the correct username.
2. Check the seller role assigned to that account.
3. Verify the page being opened is allowed for that role.
4. Escalate through the existing support channel if the route still fails.
